About Me

Hey, I am Mmesoma Onwusika from Lagos, Nigeria schooling in the US. I am a sophomore studying Computer Science with minors in Entrepreneurship and Management Information Systems. I am currently enrolled at Worcester Polytechnic Institute. I have a passion for software development and design. I’m especially passionate about front-end development, UX/UI design, and using technology to tell stories. Whether I’m working on a portfolio website, a virtual timeline app, or learning Swift, I thrive on building experiences that are both functional and beautiful. Outside of coding, I enjoy designing in Figma, mentoring others, and dreaming big — from launching startups to bridging the tech divide in underserved communities.
